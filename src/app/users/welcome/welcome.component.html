REGISTRATION PLATFORM GUIDE


Getting started is simple. Recommended browser: Google Chrome

Follow the link to confirm your registration. You will be asked to fill in the company details form. Once the company profile is created, you could add other colleagues from your company to join and to have access to all important information concerning your participation (Profile -> User management -> Plus Button to add a colleague).

Choose your preferred way of presenting Your company at the expo, e.g. booking an area for your own fair booth (Orders -> Exhibition area). Only 1 person from your company the so called “Authorized Exhibitor” could confirm the booth and/or the area and could order it. You can transfer the “authorization right” to any additionally registered colleague from your company at Profile -> User Management -> Edit -> Role -> choose Exhibitor or Authorized Exhibitor.

Additional Fair Services and Advertising Services could be ordered from the menus under Orders.

Please send your booth/area orders by the 1st of May 2018 at the latest.

Plan of the Expo with location of the booth and confirmation of the booth size will be delivered after 01.06.2018.

Places will be distributed in the same order as applications reach Inter Cars.

There is a 30% discount of all prices included and is valid up to 01.05.2018. If you have additional question our expo team is available at your services at bg-expo@intercars.eu.



EVENT GUIDE

        The location of the fair is the international exhibition center in the beautiful city of Plovdiv, 130 km away from the capital Sofia. You could find more information regarding the accommodation under Orders -> Other Services. We have prepared a storage place for your marketing materials and you can send them to Plovdiv as soon as you like, more information under Orders -> Other Services.

Here is why the expo day 30.06.2018 is a very special and important day for us:

        - It will be the first entirely B2B expo in Bulgaria and we are excited to welcome 5000 visitors, all of them coming from workshops and spare parts shops at a lifetime experience with an extraordinary Motor Show and a big concert in the evening.

        - As a market leader we will try to provide the biggest ever parts exhibition in Bulgaria, it will be the only one for 2018 and probably for the next few years in terms of number of exhibitors, number of professional customers, number of professional business entities and area.

        - The expo will be our introduction, leading towards our next big step – the start of the central warehouse of Inter Cars for Bulgaria and Greece in the autumn.

        You are welcome to use the opportunity to present your brand/s on a national level in front of the professional aftermarket in Bulgaria. The exhibition is dedicated also to the big workshops and shops, that still do not use the services of Inter Cars.
